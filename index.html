My Portfolio

Below are a few of my most recent projects that showcase my creative side. Working on these projects has been a rewarding experience that allowed me to explore new programming tools and grow as a game developer. I hope you’ll enjoy viewing my projects as much as I enjoyed working on them. Feel free to browse through my work and let me know if you'd like to know more about them.

Ruka

A turn-based puzzle game. Venture through a spooky mansion to find your way to the end where darkness awaits.

For Traum

A fast-paced 2.5D platformer. In the planet’s last city, you are tasked with quelling an active rebellion by any means necessary to ensure the survival of the human race.

Galaxy War

A top-down shooter game. Fly past and defeat waves of enemies to reach the final boss.

Frozen City

Control a flame-propelled ship to fly through a minecraft world that is frozen in time.

Counseling App

Seek out counseling by creating blog posts on a forum or chatting priavately with certified counselors.

Octree Demo

A demonstration of a multi-level octree.

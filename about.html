About Me

I am an aspiring game developer and artist based in Cupertino, California. I am currently a first year Master’s student in the Computer Science Games department at the University of Southern California. In 2018, I graduated from San José State University with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. My work is focused on combining the fields of art and programming to create games that give users an immersive and exciting gameplay experience. I have an avid interest in making games, playing games, as well as illustrating game art. I believe that through programming, we software developers have the power to take lead in the entertainment industry and serve an impactful purpose to society, whether our games help people relieve stress, learn new skills, or simply serve as a pastime. I am also a strong advocate of women in engineering and aim to help other women feel confident in joining this exciting field of study. Please feel free to take a look at my projects, and don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.

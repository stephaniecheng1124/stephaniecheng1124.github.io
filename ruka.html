Ruka

Created by Stephanie Cheng and Tony Quach

The game revolves around a young girl named Ruka, who receives a letter from her estranged grandpa telling her to come to his manor. It is a turn-based puzzle game that involves strategically traversing tile-by tile through a spooky mansion. The art style used in this game is 48 x48 resolution pixel art, and the color scheme is dark and spooky. She needs to get to her destination before she gets spooked out and faints.
